From Sketch to Screen

Every great digital product starts with a humble piece of paper. At St. Clair Design Co., we resist the urge to jump straight into software. We start by drawing.

The Thinking Hand

Sketching allows for rapid iteration. It separates the logic of the layout from the aesthetics of the design. By solving the user flow on paper first, we ensure that the foundation is solid before we ever write a line of code.

This process ensures that our final code—clean, semantic, and performant—is built on a strategy, not just a guess.
